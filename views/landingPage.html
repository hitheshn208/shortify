Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Shortify

How long is a Shortify link?

Shortify links use 6-8 character codes, making them short, memorable, and easy to type. For example: shortify.co/abc123

Can I see who clicked my link?

You get real-time click counts for every link. Detailed analytics including location, device, and referrer data are coming soon.

How do I password-protect a link?

When creating a link, toggle "Protect with password" and set your password. Recipients will enter it before accessing the full URL.

Can I download the QR code?

Yes. Every link includes a downloadable QR code. Perfect for posters, business cards, and any offline marketing materials.

Is there a limit on how many links I can create?

Create unlimited links on our free plan. There's no restriction on quantity or total clicks tracked.

Do links ever expire?

No, links are permanent by default. They'll remain active and trackable indefinitely unless you delete them.
